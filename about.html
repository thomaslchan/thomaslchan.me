About


I hail from Wayland, a small town in Massachusetts. (There's actually a display server protocol named after it.) I graduated from the local high school and now attend Tufts University in Medford, MA.

I am a senior with an intended B.S. in Biology and Computer Science. I am interested in the intersections of human health and technology — namely computational biology and translational informatics.

In my spare time, I practice film and digital photography . You can also find me reading feminist and critical race theory on my phone. Recently, I started doing tai chi and meditation.

Some of my more general interests include: vegetarian cooking, non-English music, film analysis and review, and German Shepherd dogs.
