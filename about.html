About


I am an aspiring computational biologist. I'm interested in the intersections of medicine and technology, particularly the use of software-based approaches in human disease research.

I have prior experience in building visualization tools for biological data. I am now working with single cell 'omics data to study cancer immunology at Mass General Hospital.

In my spare time, I shoot both film and digital photography. I also enjoy reading and writing fiction (especially while using public transportation).

Some of my more general interests include: long-form journalism, sustainable agriculture, teaching and German Shepherd dogs.
