Teaching


I worked as a teaching assistant during my undergraduate studies at Tufts University.


COMP170: Theory of Computation

September 2019 - January 2020

I co-led recitations of about 25 students, and I graded homeworks and the final exam. I also held office hours attended by about 10 students. I also reviewed homework materials before they were sent to students.

Topics included: models of computation, finite automata, regular languages, context free grammars, Turing machines, undecidability, diagonalizations, nondeterminism, etc.


COMP15: Data Structures

February 2019 - May 2019

I assisted with a laboratory class of around 20 students and graded projects and exams. I also hosted office hours attended by upwards of 30 students. Programming assignments were written in C++.

Topics included: abstraction, software design, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, heaps, sorting algorithms, graphs, and hashing.


COMP40: Machine Structure and Assembly Language

September 2018 - January 2019

I graded design documents for assigned projects and aided about 20 students during office hours. Programming assignments were written in C.

Topics included: memory, caches, registers, machine arithmetic, bitwise operations, and the structure of/interaction between assembly code, relocatable object code, and binary machine code.
